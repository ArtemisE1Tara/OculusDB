Downgrading quest games

1. Log in on oculus.com

Simply open oculus.com and log in with your Oculus or Facebook account.

2. Search the game you want to downgrade

Search {gamecode} in the search box at the top right corner.
Click the details button on {game}. Make sure that {game} is for your headset (e. g. Quest)

3. Download the desired version

Scroll down and click on Versions. Then click the download button on the version you want.
If you get an error saying site not found make sure that you own {game} and are logging in.

4. Uninstall {game} via Sidequest

In Sidequest (setup guide can be found here) click the 9 squares in the top right corner.
Then find {gamecode} and click the cog on the right. In the pop up press Uninstall App
This step is necessary so step 5 works

5. Install the downgraded version

Find the apk downloaded in steap 3 and drag and drop it into SideQuest.
Alternitavely press the box with a down arrow at the top right in SideQuest and select the APK.

Congrats. You now downgraded your game
